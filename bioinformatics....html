Integrative-MLR/Docking-Study-of-Benzothiazole-Derivatives

This project combines Quantitative Structure/Activity Relationship (QSAR) modeling and Molecular Docking to evaluate benzothiazole-based compounds as potential therapeutic agents against prostate cancer. Using an integrated chemoinformatics and structural-bioinformatics workflow, the study predicts biological activity, identifies promising lead compounds, and compares their binding behavior to experimentally validated native ligands.
